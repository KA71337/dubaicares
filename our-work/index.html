Our Work

Home > Our Work

Dubai Cares is playing a key role in helping achieve the United Nations Sustainable Development Goal (SDG) 4, which aims to ensure inclusive and quality education for all and promote lifelong learning by 2030, by supporting programs designed to reduce, if not remove, the greatest obstacles that prevent children and young people in developing countries from attaining quality education. 

Dubai Cares’ holistic approach focuses on four key areas: innovative education programs, evidence-based research, impactful advocacy, and timely emergency response. These pillars form the foundation of its mission to empower children and young people, communities, and countries through the transformative power of education.
