Support Us

home / Support Us

Education has the power to change the course of a life and shape the future of entire communities. Yet for millions of children around the world, this life-changing opportunity remains out of reach, not because they lack talent, dreams, or determination, but because they lack access.

Dubai Cares works to change this reality, strengthened by the unwavering support of the UAE community. Through your support, whether you choose to donate, fundraise, sponsor initiatives, or partner with us, you help turn dreams into reality for children and young people, empowering them to learn, grow, and contribute positively to their communities. 

Explore the many ways you can support Dubai Cares and be part of a global effort to make quality education a reality for every child, everywhere.
