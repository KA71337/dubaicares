Join Dubai Cares in Creating Lasting Impact Through Volunteering

At Dubai Cares, volunteering isn’t just about giving time, it’s about being part of a movement that’s transforming lives through education and community support. Our volunteering experiences are hands-on, high-impact, and deeply rewarding, offering individuals a chance to contribute to real change, both locally and globally.

Throughout the year, we host a variety of opportunities through our flagship initiatives like Volunteer Emirates, Volunteer Globally, and Walk for Education. From packing school kits to refurbishing schools, every role plays a part in delivering hope and opportunity where it’s needed most.

Spots fill up fast, often within an hour of announcement, so make sure you’re in the loop by subscribing to our volunteer database. To participate, volunteers must be registered with both the UAE Volunteer Platform and the Community Development Authority (CDA).

Stay connected with us on social media for the latest announcements, stories, and upcoming events.
